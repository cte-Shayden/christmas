Gaming Desktop Computer

A PC that can run heavy games smoothly and provide much more storage than my current laptop. Ideal for AAA titles, streaming, and content creation.

Why I want it

This desktop would replace my aging laptop for gaming and content creation. It would enable stable frame rates, faster load times, and the ability to stream or record gameplay while playing.

Key Features

  • Dedicated GPU for modern games (NVIDIA/AMD)
  • Fast NVMe SSD storage
  • 16GB+ RAM (upgradeable)
  • Good airflow and cooling

Suggested Specs

  • CPU: AMD Ryzen 5 / Intel i5 or better
  • GPU: NVIDIA GTX 1660 Super or RTX 3060 (or equivalent)
  • RAM: 16GB DDR4
  • Storage: 500GB NVMe SSD + 1TB HDD
  • OS: Windows 10/11

Priority

High — core item for gaming and productivity.
